# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are a small, professional engineering team building GLASS Padel. We are
committed to a working environment that is respectful, direct and free of
harassment, for everyone who contributes.

## Expected behaviour

- Treat colleagues and contributors with respect and good faith.
- Give and accept feedback on the work, not the person.
- Be clear and honest; disagree openly and settle it on the merits.
- Respect confidentiality around code, credentials and commercial matters.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without their consent.
- Sharing organisation secrets, credentials or private code outside the team.
- Sustained disruption of the team's work.

## Scope

This code applies within all organisation spaces, including repositories, issues,
pull requests and any communication conducted on behalf of the organisation.

## Reporting

Concerns may be raised privately with the organisation owner. Reports are handled
discreetly and in good faith. The owner is responsible for clarifying and
enforcing this code, and may take any action deemed appropriate, up to and
including removal from the organisation.
